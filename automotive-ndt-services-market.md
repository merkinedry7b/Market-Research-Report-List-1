<p><p>The automotive NDT services market comprises various testing methods to ensure vehicle safety and integrity. Ultrasonic Testing employs high-frequency sound waves to detect internal flaws, while Radiography Testing uses X-rays to visualize internal structures. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects in ferromagnetic materials through magnetic fields, and Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface discontinuities by using a penetrant that highlights flaws. Other methods may include eddy current testing and visual inspections, enhancing overall quality assurance in automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
