<p><p>The Disposable Hydrocephalus Shunt Market is categorized into two main types: stationary and adjustable shunts. Stationary shunts are pre-set devices designed to maintain a consistent cerebrospinal fluid flow, providing a reliable solution for hydrocephalus management. In contrast, adjustable shunts offer the flexibility to modify fluid drainage based on patient-specific needs, allowing for improved management of intracranial pressure. Both types serve critical roles in treating hydrocephalus, with varying applications depending on patient condition and physician preferences.</p></p>
