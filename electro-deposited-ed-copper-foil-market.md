<p><p>The electro-deposited (ED) copper foil market comprises three main types: HTE (High-Temperature Electrodeposited), STD (Standard), and DSTF (Double-Sided Thin Film) copper foil. HTE copper foil is designed for high-performance applications requiring thermal stability. STD copper foil is commonly used in various electronic applications for its balanced properties. DSTF copper foil features a thin layer suitable for advanced electronic components, providing excellent conductivity and reliability in compact designs, catering to the growing demand for miniaturized electronics.</p></p>
