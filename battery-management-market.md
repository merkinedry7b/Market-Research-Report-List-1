<p><strong>The Battery Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centralized BMS</li><li>Distributed BMS</li><li>Modular BMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Management System (BMS) market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Centralized BMS**: This system uses a single control unit for multiple battery cells, providing cost-effective management but limited scalability and flexibility.</p><p>2. **Distributed BMS**: In this setup, individual control units manage sections of the battery pack. This enhances scalability and fault tolerance, allowing for better performance monitoring.</p><p>3. **Modular BMS**: Combining elements of centralized and distributed systems, modular BMS features interchangeable modules that offer scalability and customization for various applications.</p></p>
