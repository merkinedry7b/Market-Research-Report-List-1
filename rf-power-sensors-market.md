<p><strong>The RF Power Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heat-based</li><li>Diode Detector-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RF power sensors are essential for measuring radio frequency signals, and they primarily fall into two types: heat-based and diode detector-based sensors. Heat-based sensors operate by measuring the thermal energy generated when RF power is absorbed, offering high accuracy and power handling capabilities but typically have slower response times. In contrast, diode detector-based sensors utilize semiconductor diodes to convert RF signals into a measurable voltage, providing fast response times and compact designs, making them ideal for various applications in telecommunications and broadcasting.</p></p>
