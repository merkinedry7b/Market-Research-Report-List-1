<p><p>Industrial circular connectors are categorized by their mounting types, each serving specific applications. Cable mount connectors are designed for connecting cables, offering flexibility in installation. Flange mount connectors allow secure attachment to panels, ensuring stability in harsh environments. Panel mount connectors are ideal for access points on equipment, facilitating easy connectivity. PCB mount connectors are embedded directly onto printed circuit boards, providing compact and reliable connections. Each type serves distinct purposes in various industrial and electronic applications, enhancing connectivity and performance.</p></p>
