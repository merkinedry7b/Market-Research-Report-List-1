<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The High Power Fiber Laser market primarily includes Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Lasers and Pulsed Fiber Lasers. CW Fiber Lasers emit a constant beam of light, ideal for applications requiring sustained energy like cutting and welding metals. In contrast, Pulsed Fiber Lasers release energy in short bursts, allowing for precise material processing, engraving, and marking. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, with CW lasers excelling in high-speed operations, while pulsed lasers are favored for intricate work requiring high precision.</p></p>
