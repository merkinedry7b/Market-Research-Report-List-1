<p><ul><li>Single Acting Hydraulic Rescue Spreader</li><li>Double Acitng Hydraulic Rescue Spreader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic rescue spreader market primarily features two types: single-acting and double-acting hydraulic rescue spreaders. Single-acting spreaders utilize hydraulic pressure to operate in one direction, requiring manual retraction, making them simpler but often slower in operation. In contrast, double-acting spreaders allow for hydraulic movement in both directions, enabling quicker and more efficient rescues. These tools are essential for first responders to extricate victims from vehicles or debris, enhancing safety and operational effectiveness in emergency situations.</p></p>
