<p><p>Electrical Discharge Machining (EDM) services encompass various techniques used for precision machining. Sinker EDM involves shaping complex geometries by removing material from a workpiece using a shaped electrode. Wire EDM utilizes a thin, electrically charged wire to cut through metal, ideal for intricate designs and tight tolerances. Hole Drilling EDM specializes in creating precise holes, often in hard materials, using a tube-shaped electrode to deliver electrical discharges. Each method offers unique advantages tailored for specific machining needs in manufacturing.</p></p>
