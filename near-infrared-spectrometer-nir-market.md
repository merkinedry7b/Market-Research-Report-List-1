<p><p>The Near Infrared Spectrometer (NIR) market includes various types, each serving specific analytical purposes. FT-NIR (Fourier Transform Near Infrared) utilizes interferometry for high-resolution spectral data, ideal for complex samples. UV-Vis-NIR combines ultraviolet and visible light analysis, providing a broad spectral range for applications in chemistry and material science. Raman spectroscopy, while primarily a molecular technique, complements NIR by offering insights into vibrational modes. Other types may include handheld devices and specific application-oriented systems, catering to diverse industries like pharmaceuticals and agriculture.</p></p>
