<p><ul><li>Standard Translucency (ST)</li><li>High Translucency</li><li>Ultra High Translucency (UHT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Zirconia Block market is categorized into three types based on translucency levels: Standard Translucency (ST), High Translucency, and Ultra High Translucency (UHT). ST blocks offer basic aesthetic properties suitable for posterior restorations, while High Translucency blocks provide improved aesthetics for anterior applications, delivering a balance between strength and natural appearance. UHT blocks are developed for maximum translucency, ideal for applications needing superior aesthetics, such as veneers and anterior crowns, ensuring lifelike restorations without compromising structural integrity.</p></p>
