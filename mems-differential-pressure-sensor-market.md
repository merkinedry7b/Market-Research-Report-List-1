<p><p>The MEMS differential pressure sensor market is predominantly categorized into two types: digital and analog differential pressure sensors. Digital differential pressure sensors convert pressure readings into a digital format, enabling precise measurements and easy integration with digital systems, making them ideal for modern applications. In contrast, analog differential pressure sensors provide continuous voltage or current outputs proportional to pressure changes, offering simplicity and reliability in traditional applications. Both types serve various industries, including automotive, healthcare, and HVAC, addressing diverse measurement requirements.</p></p>
