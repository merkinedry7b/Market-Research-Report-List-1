<p><p>The solid-phase oligonucleotide synthesis market encompasses several types, including primers, probes, intermediate-scale synthesis oligos, large-scale synthesis oligos, linkers, and adaptors. Primers are short sequences used in PCR amplification, while probes assist in detection and quantification of nucleic acids. Intermediate-scale synthesis oligos cater to research needs, whereas large-scale synthesis oligos are produced for commercial applications and diagnostics. Linkers and adaptors facilitate the attachment of DNA fragments, playing a crucial role in various molecular biology techniques.</p></p>
