<p><ul><li>Horizontal Lined Pumps</li><li>Vertical Lined Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lined pumps market primarily consists of two types: horizontal lined pumps and vertical lined pumps. Horizontal lined pumps are designed for efficient fluid movement in a horizontal orientation, making them ideal for applications requiring high flow rates and ease of maintenance. In contrast, vertical lined pumps are optimized for confined spaces and are often used in situations where vertical lift is necessary, such as in tanks or pits. Both types are critical in industries demanding corrosion resistance and reliability.</p></p>
