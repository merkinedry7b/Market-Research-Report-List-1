<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Through Beam Laser Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NPN</li><li>PNP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Through beam laser sensors are categorized into NPN and PNP types based on their output configuration. NPN sensors, which are generally named 'sinking' sensors, allow current to flow from the load to ground when activated, making them suitable for applications that require a low signal. In contrast, PNP sensors, or 'sourcing' sensors, allow current to flow from the power supply to the load when triggered, ideal for systems needing a high signal. Both types are essential for various automation and detection applications.</p></p>
