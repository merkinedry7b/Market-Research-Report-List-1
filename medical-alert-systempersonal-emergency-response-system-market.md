<p><p>A Medical Alert System, also known as a Personal Emergency Response System (PERS), is a technology designed to provide immediate assistance to individuals in medical emergencies. Additionally, the demand for wireless communication and remote monitoring has led to the development of more sophisticated systems, capable of providing enhanced features like fall detection and GPS tracking.
