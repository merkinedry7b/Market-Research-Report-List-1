<p><p>The paper bowl machine market primarily focuses on two types of lamination: single-sided and double-sided PE lamination. Single-sided PE lamination involves applying a polyethylene coating to only one side of the paper, making it suitable for items that don't require heavy moisture protection. Each type serves different market needs based on usage and performance requirements.</p></p>
