<p><ul><li>Externally-Threaded</li><li>Internal Thread</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cryogenic vial market is categorized into externally-threaded and internally-threaded vials, each serving specific needs in laboratory and storage applications. Externally-threaded vials feature threads that extend outside the vial, allowing for secure closures and compatibility with a variety of caps. In contrast, internally-threaded vials have threads located within the vial's neck, providing a more streamlined design that can minimize contamination risks. Both types are essential for effectively storing biological samples at ultra-low temperatures, ensuring sample integrity and safety.</p></p>
