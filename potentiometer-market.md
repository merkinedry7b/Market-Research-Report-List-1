<p><p>The potentiometer market is divided into rotary and linear types, each serving distinct applications. Rotary potentiometers adjust resistance through a circular motion and are commonly used in volume controls and adjustment settings in various devices. Linear potentiometers, on the other hand, vary resistance by sliding a contact along a straight path, frequently utilized in applications like position sensing or measurement systems. Both types are essential in electronic circuits for controlling voltage and signal levels in diverse industries.</p></p>
