<p><p>The ablation catheters market is divided into two primary types: multielectrode and single point ablation catheters. Multielectrode ablation catheters feature multiple electrodes, allowing for simultaneous delivery of energy to multiple sites, enhancing treatment efficiency and precision. Conversely, single point ablation catheters focus energy delivery on one site at a time, providing targeted therapy for specific areas. Both types are crucial in cardiac and tissue ablation procedures, with specific applications determined by the complexity and requirements of the treatment.</p></p>
