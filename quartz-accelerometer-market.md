<p><ul><li>Single Axis</li><li>Multi-axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Quartz Accelerometer market is divided into single-axis and multi-axis types. Single-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along one specific direction, making them suitable for basic applications like tilt sensing. In contrast, multi-axis accelerometers can measure acceleration along multiple directions simultaneously, offering more comprehensive data for complex applications such as navigation and robotics. The choice between the two types depends on the specific requirements of the application, including factors like accuracy, sensitivity, and the environment in which they will operate.</p></p>
