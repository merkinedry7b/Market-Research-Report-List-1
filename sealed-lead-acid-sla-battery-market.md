<p><p>The Sealed Lead Acid (SLA) battery market includes two main types: Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) and Gel batteries. AGM batteries utilize a glass mat to absorb electrolyte, offering high discharge rates and low resistance, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, Gel batteries contain liquid electrolyte in a gel form, providing safe operation and resistance to extreme temperatures. Both types are popular in various applications, including renewable energy, telecommunications, and emergency systems, due to their reliability and maintenance-free design.</p></p>
