<p><strong>The Manual Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Control Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual valve market includes several types, each serving distinct purposes. Gate valves are ideal for on/off applications, providing minimal flow resistance. Control valves regulate fluid flow and pressure, essential for process management. Ball valves offer quick shut-off with a quarter-turn mechanism, suitable for various fluids. Butterfly valves are space-efficient for large volumes, utilizing a rotating disc to control flow. Together, these valves cater to diverse industrial needs, ensuring effective fluid management across applications.</p></p>
