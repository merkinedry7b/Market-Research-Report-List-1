<p><ul><li>Ceramic Oxide Fiber</li><li>Ceramic Non-Oxide Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramic fiber fabric market consists of two main types: ceramic oxide fibers and ceramic non-oxide fibers. Ceramic oxide fibers, primarily made from alumina-silica compounds, offer excellent thermal resistance and stability, making them ideal for high-temperature applications. In contrast, ceramic non-oxide fibers, such as silicon carbide and boron nitride, are known for their exceptional strength and lower thermal conductivity, suitable for specialized environments. Both types cater to industries requiring lightweight, insulation materials and thermal protection solutions.</p></p>
