<p><p>The Virtual Meeting Software market can be categorized into two main types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise solutions. Cloud-Based software is hosted on the vendor's servers, offering scalability, easy access from any device, and lower upfront costs, making it ideal for remote teams. In contrast, On-Premise solutions are installed locally on a company's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization but requiring higher initial investments and maintenance. Organizations choose between these types based on their specific needs and infrastructure.</p></p>
