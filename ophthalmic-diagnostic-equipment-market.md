<p><p>The ophthalmic diagnostic equipment market encompasses various tools essential for eye examination and diagnosis. Fundus cameras capture detailed images of the retina, while ophthalmoscopes allow for direct visualization of the eye's interior. Refractors measure vision refractive errors, and Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides high-resolution cross-sectional images of retinal structures. Corneal topography systems analyze the curvature of the cornea, slit lamps offer magnified views of the eye's anterior segment, tonometers measure intraocular pressure, perimeters assess visual fields, and retinal ultrasound imaging provides insights into deeper structures of the eye.</p></p>
