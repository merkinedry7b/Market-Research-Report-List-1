<p><p>The Outdoor Emergency Alert System (OEAS) market is segmented into omni-directional and rotating directional types. Omni-directional systems broadcast alerts in all directions, ensuring widespread coverage and immediacy in communication during emergencies. In contrast, rotating directional systems focus sound in specific directions by using rotating speakers, enabling targeted alerts that can cover larger distances while minimizing sound pollution in non-critical areas. Both types serve crucial roles in urban and rural settings, enhancing public safety and awareness during emergencies.</p></p>
