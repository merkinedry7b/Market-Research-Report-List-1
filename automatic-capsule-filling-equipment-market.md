<p><p>The automatic capsule filling equipment market is primarily divided into two types: continuous type and intermittent type. Continuous type machines operate in a constant flow, allowing for higher production rates and efficiency, making them ideal for large-scale manufacturing. In contrast, intermittent type machines operate in cycles, filling capsules one batch at a time, offering flexibility and precision for smaller production runs or diverse product variations. Both types cater to different industry needs based on volume and operational requirements.</p></p>
