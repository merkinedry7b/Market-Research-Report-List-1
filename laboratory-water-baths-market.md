<p><ul><li>Circulating Water Baths</li><li>Non-Circulating Water Baths</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory water baths market is divided into two main types: circulating and non-circulating water baths. Circulating water baths feature a pump that ensures consistent temperature distribution by continuously moving water, making them ideal for precise applications. In contrast, non-circulating water baths maintain stable temperatures but lack the circulation feature, making them suitable for simpler tasks. Both types are widely used in various laboratories for applications such as incubation, heating, and sample preparation, tailored to specific experimental needs.</p></p>
