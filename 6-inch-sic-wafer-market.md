<p><p>The 6-inch SiC wafer market consists of two main types: conductive SiC wafers and semi-insulating SiC wafers. Conductive SiC wafers are primarily used in high-power electronics, such as inverters and power supplies, due to their excellent electrical conductivity and thermal properties. In contrast, semi-insulating SiC wafers serve applications that require isolation, like RF devices and high-frequency components, due to their ability to minimize leakage currents. Each type caters to specific industry needs, driving growth in various sectors.</p></p>
