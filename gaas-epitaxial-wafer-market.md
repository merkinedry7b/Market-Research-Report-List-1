<p><p>The GaAs epitaxial wafer market is primarily segmented into three types: MOCVD (Metal-Organic Chemical Vapor Deposition), MBE (Molecular Beam Epitaxy), and other methods. MOCVD is favored for its ability to produce high-quality and uniform films efficiently, making it prevalent in optoelectronics and power devices. MBE offers precise control over layer thickness and composition, ideal for high-performance applications. Other methods may include liquid phase epitaxy or conductive atomic force microscopy, used for niche applications or specific material requirements, contributing to market diversity.</p></p>
