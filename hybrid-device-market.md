<p><p>The hybrid device market can be categorized into two primary types: detachable and convertible hybrid devices. Detachable hybrid devices feature screens that can be separated from the keyboard, transforming into stand-alone tablets. In contrast, convertible hybrid devices have flexible hinges, allowing them to transition between laptop and tablet modes without disconnection. Both types offer versatility and portability, catering to users seeking the functionality of a laptop alongside the convenience of a tablet, making them ideal for a range of computing needs.</p></p>
