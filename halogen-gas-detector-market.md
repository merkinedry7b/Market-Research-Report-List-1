<p><ul><li>Electrochemical</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The halogen gas detector market encompasses two primary types: electrochemical and infrared detectors. Electrochemical detectors utilize chemical reactions to detect specific gases, providing high sensitivity and quick response times, making them suitable for detecting lower concentration levels of halogen gases. In contrast, infrared detectors operate by measuring the absorption of infrared light by gas molecules, allowing for non-invasive detection of higher concentrations. Both methods play crucial roles in safety applications across various industries, ensuring effective monitoring of hazardous halogen gases.</p></p>
