<p><p>The reusable laparoscopic electrodes market includes several types, each tailored for specific surgical applications. L Hook and J Hook electrodes are designed for precise tissue manipulation and cutting, providing versatility in procedures. Needle electrodes are ideal for coagulation and tissue approximation, ensuring minimal thermal spread. Spatula electrodes facilitate broader tissue contact, making them effective for dissection and fulguration. Ball electrodes are suited for coagulation and cutting, offering a rounded tip for extensive tissue handling. These specialized electrodes enhance surgical efficiency and precision.</p></p>
