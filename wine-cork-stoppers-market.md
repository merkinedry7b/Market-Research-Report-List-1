<p><p>The wine cork stoppers market comprises three main types. Natural cork stoppers, made from the bark of cork oak trees, offer quality and traditional sealing. Agglomerated cork stoppers, crafted from granulated cork particles bound together, provide a cost-effective alternative with varied densities. Capsulated cork stoppers combine natural cork with a synthetic or plastic coating, ensuring a tight seal and minimizing oxygen exposure while maintaining aesthetic appeal. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and wine preservation needs.</p></p>
