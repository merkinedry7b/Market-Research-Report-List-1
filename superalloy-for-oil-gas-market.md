<p><p>Superalloys used in the oil and gas market are classified into three main types: iron-based, nickel-based, and cobalt-based alloys. Iron-based superalloys offer good corrosion resistance and are cost-effective, making them suitable for less demanding applications. Nickel-based superalloys excel in high-temperature environments, providing superior performance under stress and corrosion, which is critical in deep-sea operations. Cobalt-based superalloys, while less common, are utilized for their exceptional strength and thermal stability, often found in extreme environments and specialized applications.</p></p>
