<p><ul><li>Plane Target</li><li>Rotating Target</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ITO sputtering target material market comprises two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, fixed surfaces used in traditional sputtering processes, suitable for various applications due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, rotating targets feature a cylindrical or disk shape that allows for continuous movement during sputtering, enhancing uniformity and efficiency in film deposition. Both target types are essential for producing indium tin oxide coatings used in electronics, displays, and optoelectronics.</p></p>
