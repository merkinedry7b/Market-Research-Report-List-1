<p><p>The surgical table market can be categorized into two main types: manual surgical tables and electrodynamic surgical tables. Manual surgical tables rely on mechanical adjustments operated by healthcare professionals to position patients during procedures. In contrast, electrodynamic surgical tables utilize electric motors and advanced technology, allowing for precise positioning and adjustments with minimal physical effort. These tables enhance operational efficiency and patient safety, catering to diverse surgical needs and improving workflow in healthcare settings.</p></p>
