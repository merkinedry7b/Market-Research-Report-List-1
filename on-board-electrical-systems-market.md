<p><p>The On-Board Electrical Systems market encompasses two primary types: On-Board AC Electrical Systems and On-Board DC Electrical Systems. On-Board AC Electrical Systems are often used in larger vehicles like buses and trains, providing reliable power for air conditioning, lighting, and appliances, using alternating current. In contrast, On-Board DC Electrical Systems are commonly found in smaller vehicles, such as cars and motorcycles, offering efficient power for ignition, battery charging, and electronic systems, using direct current for better energy management and control.</p></p>
