<p><p>The Portable Ultrasonic Flaw Detector market can be categorized by display types: A Type, M Type, and B Type. A Type displays provide a simple, one-dimensional representation of echo amplitudes, useful for identifying flaws in materials. M Type displays, or time-of-flight systems, offer a more detailed, mapping of flaws across material surfaces, allowing for better analysis. B Type displays utilize cross-sectional imaging to visualize internal structures, making them ideal for complex inspections, such as in welds or composites.</p></p>
