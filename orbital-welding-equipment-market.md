<p><p>The orbital welding equipment market encompasses various types, including pipe orbital welding, fusion orbital welding, and tube-to-tubesheet welding. Pipe orbital welding focuses on continuous, high-quality welds in piping systems, ideal for industries like oil and gas. Fusion orbital welding is designed for precise, helical welds in thin materials, commonly used in semiconductor and pharmaceutical applications. Tube-to-tubesheet welding facilitates robust connections between tubes and sheets in heat exchangers, ensuring efficient thermal transfer in power generation and chemical processes.</p></p>
