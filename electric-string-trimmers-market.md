<p><p>The electric string trimmer market is divided into two primary types: electric corded string trimmers and electric cordless string trimmers. Electric corded string trimmers require a continuous connection to a power outlet, providing unlimited run time and consistent power but limiting mobility due to cord length. In contrast, electric cordless string trimmers are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering greater maneuverability and convenience for users, making them ideal for larger areas or remote locations without easy access to electrical outlets.</p></p>
