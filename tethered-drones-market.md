<p><ul><li>Ground Fixed Type</li><li>Vehicle Mounted Mobile Type</li><li>Shipborne Mobile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tethered drones market comprises three main types: Ground Fixed Type, Vehicle Mounted Mobile Type, and Shipborne Mobile Type. Ground Fixed Type drones are stationary units anchored to a specific location for surveillance or monitoring. Vehicle Mounted Mobile Type drones are integrated into ground vehicles, allowing for mobility while providing aerial capabilities during operations. Shipborne Mobile Type drones are deployed from marine vessels, enabling maritime surveillance and reconnaissance, and are especially useful for coastal monitoring and search-and-rescue missions.</p></p>
