<p><p>The laser alignment tool market is categorized primarily into two types: single laser technology and dual laser technology. Single laser technology utilizes one laser beam to establish alignment, making it suitable for basic tasks and smaller projects. In contrast, dual laser technology employs two laser beams, allowing for enhanced accuracy and versatility in complex alignment tasks across larger areas. Dual laser systems are often preferred in industrial applications due to their ability to provide real-time feedback and improved precision during alignment processes.</p></p>
