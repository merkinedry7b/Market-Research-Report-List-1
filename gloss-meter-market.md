<p><p>The gloss meter market is segmented based on measurement angles: single angle, two angles, and multi angles. Single angle gloss meters measure reflectivity at one fixed angle, suitable for standard finishes. Two-angle gloss meters offer measurements at two angles, providing a more comprehensive assessment of surface gloss. Multi-angle gloss meters, on the other hand, can measure at several angles, giving detailed insights into surface characteristics across a wider range of finishes, making them ideal for diverse applications in various industries.</p></p>
