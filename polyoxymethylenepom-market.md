<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) is a highly crystalline thermoplastic known for its strength, stiffness, and dimensional stability. The market for POM is categorized into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene is characterized by its uniform chemical structure, offering excellent mechanical properties and resistance to chemicals. In contrast, co-polyoxymethylene is synthesized from two different monomers, enhancing flexibility and impact resistance. Both types find applications in automotive, electronics, and industrial sectors, catering to specific performance requirements.</p></p>
