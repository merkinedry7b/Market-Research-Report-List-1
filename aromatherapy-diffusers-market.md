<p><strong>The Aromatherapy Diffusers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Nebulizer</li><li>Evaporative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aromatherapy diffusers market includes three main types: ultrasonic, nebulizer, and evaporative. Ultrasonic diffusers use high-frequency vibrations to mix water and essential oils, creating a fine mist. Nebulizers, on the other hand, atomize essential oils without water, delivering a stronger concentration of aroma. Evaporative diffusers rely on a fan to blow air through a pad soaked in essential oils, promoting natural evaporation. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to different preferences and spaces in aromatherapy practices.</p></p>
