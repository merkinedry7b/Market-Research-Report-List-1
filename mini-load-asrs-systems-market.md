<p><ul><li>Single Shuttle System</li><li>Dual Shuttle System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mini-Load AS/RS (Automated Storage and Retrieval Systems) are designed to efficiently handle small to medium-sized loads. The Single Shuttle System utilizes one shuttle to move horizontally along a track, retrieving and placing items, ideal for lower throughput operations. In contrast, the Dual Shuttle System employs two shuttles working simultaneously, enhancing speed and productivity by allowing parallel operations. This makes the dual system suitable for high-volume environments, providing improved efficiency, reduced cycle times, and optimized storage density.</p></p>
