<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The HVAC Air Handling Units Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Draw Through Type</li><li>Blow Through Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC air handling unit (AHU) market segments primarily into Draw Through and Blow Through types. Draw Through units pull air through the filters and heating/cooling coils before pushing it into the ductwork, ensuring better filtration and efficiency. Conversely, Blow Through units push air through the coils and filters, often providing higher airflow but potentially less effective filtration. Each type is designed for specific applications, impacting energy efficiency, maintenance needs, and installation preferences in building environments.</p></p>
