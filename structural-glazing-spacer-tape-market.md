<p><p>The structural glazing spacer tape market comprises two main types: double-sided and single-sided tape. Double-sided tape features adhesive on both sides, allowing for secure attachment between glass and framing materials, enhancing structural integrity and insulation. In contrast, single-sided tape has adhesive on only one side, primarily used for sealing or as a temporary fix. Both types serve critical roles in construction and architectural applications, ensuring durability, energy efficiency, and moisture protection in structural glazing projects.</p></p>
